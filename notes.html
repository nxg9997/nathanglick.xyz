Above and Beyond

The first thing most users will see when visiting my site (unless you are on mobile) is my autoplaying video of a few games that I have created. This also doubles as a parallax layer, which the main content of the page will slide over when you scroll. Next, in the gallery, if you click a thumbnail of a game, a popup div will display showing details about the game. This is done by using javascript to capture clicks on the images. Clicking the popup will make it disappear.
